About us

Ryan & Tatiana Hoek founded Terriana with the goal to create aesthetically appealing water wise landscapes that are unique, and take your breath away.

Thank you for your interest in Terriana! We are landscape design and construction experts in the Bay Area of California, with a focus on creating unique and functional outdoor spaces tailored to your interests and budget. We’ve been fortunate to be part of incredible projects. Our process begins by listening to our clients' needs, after which we present two design options. We then discuss and make any necessary adjustments before moving on to the construction phase.
