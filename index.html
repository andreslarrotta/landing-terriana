At Terriana, we design and build landscapes in California with a sustainable approach, promoting the use of native plants to preserve and create ecosystems. The species we utilize require less water, encourage biodiversity, and create natural habitats for the native wildlife! Our goal is to integrate design and nature to create functional, aesthetic, and durable spaces.
